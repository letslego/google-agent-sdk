FAQ: Claim API latency
- If latency exceeds 500ms p95 for over 15 minutes, classify as P1 for enterprise customers.
- Recommended mitigation sequence: enable cache warmup, scale workers, and route to nearest healthy region.

FAQ: Invoice mismatch
- Reconcile usage snapshots from billing warehouse before issuing corrected invoice.
- For EMEA accounts, include VAT adjustment note in customer response.

FAQ: Webhook retries
- Validate endpoint health and signature verification.
- Retry policy: exponential backoff up to 8 attempts over 20 minutes.
