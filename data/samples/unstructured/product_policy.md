# Enterprise Support Policy

Platinum tier customers receive a 99.95% uptime SLA and 24x7 P1 response within 15 minutes.

For high-priority incidents involving customer-facing APIs:
1. Open incident bridge within 10 minutes.
2. Provide customer status updates every 30 minutes.
3. Publish root-cause analysis within 5 business days after mitigation.

Data retention for support logs is 13 months unless stricter contractual requirements apply.
